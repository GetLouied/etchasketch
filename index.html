Grid Genius

Usage Keys:

Left Click

Clicking or holding the left mouse button changes the pixels to the selected color.

Left Click + Shift

Clicking or holding the left mouse button + shift darkens the pixels.

Right Click

Clicking or holding the right mouse button resets the pixels to white.

Right Click + Shift

Clicking or holding the right mouse button + shift lightens the pixels.
